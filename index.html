Intro

I'm on a thrilling data adventure, weaving captivating narratives from the numbers! Currently navigating the world of Cargo Airline business at Cargojet Canada, I made the leap from Nigeria to Ontario in April 2022, bringing my passion for data along for the ride.

My data journey kicked off in a public sector consulting firm, where I spent over 3 years crafting automated solutions to tackle challenges faced by state governments across Nigeria. This experience fueled my love for data, prompting me to pursue a graduate degree in Analytics for Business Decision Making while simultaneously rocking a Masterschool Program on Data Analytics. Who says you can't do it all? Certainly not me!

In the midst of this data-fueled frenzy, I clinched the 2nd spot in the 2023 HEAD (Higher Education Analytics Data) Competition, triumphing over other data and business analyst students in Ontario's colleges.

My data toolbox is brimming with Python scripts for dazzling data cleaning and transformation. For the smaller datasets, I wield the mighty power of MS Excel and Google Sheets, unleashing Pivot Tables, PowerQuery, DAX and logical wizardry to cook up advanced analyses. And when it's time to create dynamic dashboards, I dance between Tableau, PowerBI, and MS Excel with finesse.

Beyond the data realm, catch me indulging in the latest trends in the data industry, cheering on my favorite soccer team (Go Gunners!), binge-watching The Office (admittedly too many times), or challenging opponents in FIFA. Whether you're a data enthusiast or just here for the fun, this space is your go-to for exciting updates. Join me in the data-fun zone, where the insights are sparkling, and the code is always groovy!

GLOBAL DEFORESTATION TREND

Embarking on a quest to tackle global deforestation and spotlight its environmental impact, I delved into historical deforestation data with the aim of making a positive change. Armed with data gathered from the World Bank, I spearheaded an exhilarating exploratory data analysis, uncovering trends in forest area, total land area, and regional dynamics from 1990 to 2016.

ACCESSIBLE TRANSPORTATION SERVICE IN HAMILTON, ONTARIO

This project, my submission at the 2023 HEAD (Higher Education Analytics Data) Competition for Data Analystswhere I clinched the second-place prize delves into the profound changes faced by ATS bus services in the wake of the COVID-19 pandemic, leveraging a meticulously acquired dataset to dissect ridership patterns.

The analysis unfolds across two pivotal periods: the Pre-Covid Era (2019) and the Post-Covid Era (2022). By scrutinizing these phases, the report aims to unearth shifts in customer behavior and activities, equipping ATS with actionable insights to adapt and optimize services for the changing needs of their clientele.

US CENSUS DEMOGRAPHIC DISTRIBUTION

In this extensive data analysis project, I undertook a thorough investigation into the United States' State of Affairs, emphasizing key metrics such as unemployment, income, and transportation. Through a multifaceted analysis, the goal was to reveal correlations, distributions, and rankings, fostering a nuanced comprehension of the socio-economic landscape. The project not only presented a comprehensive overview of unemployment, income, and transportation but also delivered strategic insights essential for guiding policymakers, businesses, and communities.
